About Us

At Students for Students,

our mission is to inspire the next generation to excel!

  • We connect students from the Fremont community together.
  • By having high school students tutor elementary students, we provide a learning experience for both the tutors and the students.
  • With qualified, high-achieving tutors from all over FUSD, students will receive exceptional coaching.

The combination of these factors, building a community, creating a learning experience for both students and tutors, and providing quality tutelage, work together to help our combined generation to receive the skills needed to excel.
