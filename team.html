Rhea Shah

Co-Founder

Rhea is a computer science student at the Univeristy of Austin at Texas and co-founded SFS in 2019 in the hopes to give back to her elementary school by providing affordable, quality education to children throughout the community. She has played a huge role in growing SFS into a national program and mainly handles expenses for the organization. She also designed our popular Summer Coding Boot Camp. Outside of SFS, Rhea is a dancer on her school's Bollywood Fusion team, Texas Mohini!

Asilah Maryam

Co-Founder

Asilah is a rising junior at American High School and a co-founder of SFS. One of her core values is helping those that helped shape the person she is today, one of the main reasons she jumped at the opportunity to help found SFS at Ardenwood. Her job is to deal with communications (along with Rhea) and match students whith tutors, resolving any issues that may occur within the pairings. She also played a huge role in transferring SFS online when schools closed. Apart from SFS, Asilah is the captain of her robotics team and a proud Girl Scout. In her free time, she enjoys reading, baking, and calligraphy.

Navya Seth

Executive Board Member

Navya is a sophmore at American High, and a publicist for SFS. She loves movies, painting, animals, and has a strong passion for music, technology, and current world events. At school, Navya is a part of Speech & Debate and studies French. She has enjoyed tutoring with SFS in the past year and has helped develop the coding curriculum, make this website, and advertise for SFS. She looks forward to working with the new tutors and students, and is excited to see SFS grow!

Naina Razi

Executive Board Member

Naina Razi is the secretary for SFS tutors and is a sophomore at American High school. She is also the program manager of ECDE foundation. Naina is currently working to raise money for a school in Karnataka in desperate need of library books, sports equipment, and school supplies. Aryaman Kukal

Outside of SFS, Aryaman is the CEO of Codefy, a CS non-profit, enjoys spending time with his dog Astro, and is an avid programmer!

Ruhi Shah
