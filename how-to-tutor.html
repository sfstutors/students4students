How to Tutor with SFS?

Welcome to Students for Students!

On this page you will find everything you need to know about ‘How to Tutor’ from registration to upcoming events to how payment works.

    The first step to tutoring with SFS is filling out the tryout interest form on the Tutor Registration page.
  • You will select which subject(s) (homework help, Spanish/ French, pre-algebra) you would like to test for, and an SFS team member will email you all appropriate tests. After evaluating your scores, if you qualify, you will be notified via email. Tutors have the option to either receive 1.5 service hours per class or be paid $6 per class. The payment option is only available if you can provide some proof of having completed 18+ service learning hours already (ex. Screenshot of Infinite Campus portal).
  • SFS is adjusting to the rapidly evolving Covid-19 situation and we are currently tutoring via Zoom every Tuesday from 3:30 - 5pm. During the school year, we meet weekly on the Ardenwood Elementary premises.

Service Hours:

  • If you are receiving service hours, you are in charge of tracking them each class on a printed sheet.
  • Hours will be signed at the end of the semester, allowing you to log about 6 hours per month.
  • During COVID, hours will be scanned over to an SFS executive, and they will get them signed by our advisor.

    • Payment:

  • To become a paid tutor, you must be able to show proof of completing 18+ service hours already.
  • Payments are sent out at the end of the semester via Paypal.
  • Please contact an SFS team member if you do not have a PayPal account and we will find an alternative.
