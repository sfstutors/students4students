Course Details - French

Course Information

In this course we focus on teaching your students the key areas of listening, speaking, reading and writing skills in French. Each lesson introduces new vocabulary and grammar concepts through listening comprehension, speaking, and writing activities and learning is reinforced through periodic assessments. Students learn to talk about themselves and others, describe their surroundings and use numbers for dates and time. Regular verbs are introduced in the present tense. Simple grammatical structures are practiced in innovating and interesting ways with a variety of learning styles in mind. We will use platforms approved by teachers all around the world such as quizlet, and vhl to make sure students are engaged and actively participating as they learn. The course has been aligned to meet all standards of the French 1 courses provided by schools in the Fremont Unified School District. Kids will walk away from this course with a comprehensive understanding of the basics of the French language and culture and be prepared to excel in the course at school.
